FAQ

Short answers to common questions.

How long does a typical install take?
Most residential installs take 1 to 3 days depending on size and electrical scope.
Do you help with permits and rebates?
Yes. We handle permits and provide documents you need for rebates and incentives.
Will solar damage my roof?
A proper install uses correct flashing and mounting practices. We design for long-term roof integrity.
Can I add a battery later?
Yes. We can design the system to be battery-ready and add storage later.
